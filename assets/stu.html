Student Tracking and Data Analysis

This function plays a central role in tracking and analysing student data to enhance the Academic Strategic Plan of the institution, support the Quality Management Plan, and facilitate the Quality Review Process. It focuses on identifying 'at-risk' students, providing timely interventions, and pinpointing root causes to optimize resource allocation and promote student success.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Development and Implementation of Student Tracking System: Develop and implement a robust system to monitor student performance and access to academic resources.
  • Cohort Analyses and 'At-Risk' Identification: Analyze trends in dropout, retention, and graduation rates per cohort.
  • Intervention Strategies and Student Recognition: Apply interventions and recognize high-performing students.
  • Trends Analysis and Module Identification: Flag modules with high failure rates per faculty.
  • Predictive Analysis for Early Intervention: Forecast high-risk students early for timely intervention.
  • Resource Allocation Collaboration: Work with faculties to target support and address risk factors.
  • Access to Data and Insights: Share dashboards and reports with decision-makers for proactive intervention.

This consolidated function empowers real-time decisions that improve institutional academic strategies and student success outcomes.

Learning Analytics

The Learning Analytics function is dedicated to leveraging data from the institution's eLearning system to enhance learning, inform decisions, and optimize educational outcomes. It collects, analyzes, and reports on learner data to improve student success.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Individual Learner Insights: Equip students with dashboards for self-monitoring and growth tracking.
  • Early Identification of Students Needing Support: Use predictive models to detect academic risks early.
  • Academic and Support Staff Support: Enable effective group or individual intervention planning.
  • Curriculum Enhancement: Identify opportunities to improve content delivery and instructional design.
  • Strategic Decision-Making: Guide institutional decisions on recruitment, retention, and marketing.
  • Privacy and Data Security: Maintain strict compliance with data privacy and protection standards.

This function strengthens the learning environment by making learning data actionable for both students and educators.

Institutional Research Intelligence

The Institutional Research Intelligence function at WSU conducts regular surveys among staff and students to gather valuable information that guides planning, performance monitoring, and strategic innovation.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Conceptualization and Implementation of Research Studies: Plan and manage strategic research initiatives across WSU.
  • Institutional Surveys: Design, run, and evaluate surveys aligned with institutional goals.
  • Coordination of Research Activities: Oversee all stages of survey execution and reporting.
  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): Link survey data to institutional performance measures.
  • Strategic Advantages and Innovation: Use research to position WSU for leadership and innovation.
  • Responsive Decision-Making: Conduct timely, relevant research to inform high-level decisions and respond to trends.

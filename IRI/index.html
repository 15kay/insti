Institutional Research Intelligence

The Institutional Research Intelligence function at WSU conducts regular surveys among staff and students to gather valuable information about the institution. This information aids in institutional planning, identifies areas requiring action, and offers insights for decision-making. Additionally, the function conducts ad hoc surveys to investigate specific issues that necessitate in-depth analysis. It provides expert guidance and advice to various staff members on matters related to institutional research, particularly in the application and interpretation of survey data.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Conceptualization and Implementation of Research Studies: Develop and execute a comprehensive plan for institutional research studies across WSU. Identify research objectives, methodologies, and data collection strategies.
  • Institutional Surveys: Conduct various types of institutional surveys for academic and administrative units across WSU. Ensure the design, administration, and analysis of surveys are consistent and aligned with institutional goals.
  • Coordination of Research Activities: Establish, maintain, and coordinate WSU's institutional research activities. Oversee the collection, analysis, interpretation, and reporting of data from institutional surveys.
  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): Utilize survey data to provide insights and metrics for evaluating institutional Key Performance Indicators (KPIs). Collaborate with stakeholders to assess WSU's performance against set benchmarks.
  • Strategic Advantages and Innovation: Identify and leverage WSU's comparative advantages through data-driven insights. Promote innovation and leadership by utilising institutional research to maximize impact.
  • Responsive Decision-Making: Empower WSU to respond effectively to strategic needs and emerging trends through the application of institutional research and analytics. Stay informed about trends in higher education and conduct studies on issues impacting the university's operations and objectives.

The Institutional Research Intelligence function serves as a vital resource for WSU, enabling informed decision-making, strategic planning, and continuous improvement by providing timely and accurate data and insights.
